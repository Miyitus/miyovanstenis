DBRAGA v 2.0 x Eroticissima Game

DBRAGA is the whisper of the planets, ephemeral curls of light. She sent and protected the message of the planets by serving in her mineral form. Hostess of Eroticissima, she's a NPC powered by Inworld, a project using ChatGPT technology, allowing this character to be autonomous and cognitive with long-term memory during gameplay. DBRAGA is designed to give lifelike interactions and the ability to remember past interactions, enhancing the realism and depth on Eroticissima.
