It's Miyö, Bitch!

Born in 1989 @ Caracas, Venezuela. Based in Paris, France.

Miyö Van Stenis is an Artist, Game Developer and Curator specialized in immersive realities. Her work explores in the technological field: Immersive Environments, Crypto Market, Interfaces, Operating systems, software's and devices involved in the Internet as an performative action where the value is the human pursuing the error or the limits of the technology. Her curatorial work is centered in the criticism and the aesthetics of new medias/technologies; such as DeOrigenBelico since 2010; Beautiful Interfaces since 2013 and founder member of the activist group: Dismantling the Simulation.
